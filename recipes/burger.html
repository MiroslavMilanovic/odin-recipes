Description

This dish will be very easy to make if u follow along the recipe below

It takes only about half an hour of your day time

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 4 teaspoons Cajun seasoning, divided
  • 1 ⅓ pounds ground beef sirloin
  • ½ cup diced white onion
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and chopped
  • ...

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Combine mayonnaise and 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning in a small bowl; set aside.

  3. Combine ground sirloin, onion, remaining 3 teaspoons Cajun seasoning, jalapeño, garlic,

    and Worcestershire sauce in a large bowl. Divide into 4 balls; flatten into patties.

  4. Cook on the preheated grill until burgers are no longer pink in centers, about 5 minutes

    side, topping each burger with 1 cheese slice, during the last 2 minutes. An instant-read

    thermometer inserted into centers should read at least 160 degrees
  5. Spread mayo on buns, add burgers, lettuce, and tomatoes.
