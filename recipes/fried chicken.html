Steps

  1. In a shallow dish, mix together the garlic powder, pepper, salt, paprika, bread

    crumbs and flour. In a separate dish, whisk together the milk and egg.

  2. Heat the oil in an electric skillet set to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Dip the chicken

    into the egg and milk, then dredge in the dry ingredients until evenly coated.

  3. Fry chicken in the hot oil for about 5 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and

    juices run clear. Remove from the oil with a slotted spatula, and serve.
